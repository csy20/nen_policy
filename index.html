Nen Music Player

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Introduction

Nen ("we", "our", or "the app") is an offline music player for Android and iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information Nen accesses, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Access

Nen is designed to work entirely offline. The app accesses only the following information on your device:

  • Audio Files: Nen reads audio files stored on your device to display your music library and play songs. On Android 13 and above, this uses the READ_MEDIA_AUDIO permission. On older Android versions, the READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission is used instead. On iOS, the Media Library permission is used.
  • App Preferences: Settings you configure within the app — such as accent color, crossfade duration, playback speed, reduce motion/flash preferences, favorite songs, and recently played tracks — are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.

Information We Do Not Collect

Nen does not:

  • Collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers
  • Require user accounts, login, or registration
  • Use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks
  • Access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any sensors
  • Make any network or internet requests
  • Share any data with third parties

Permissions

The app requests the following device permissions:

Permission Purpose
READ_MEDIA_AUDIO (Android 13+) Access audio files on your device to build and display your music library
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below) Access audio files on older Android versions
FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK Allow music to continue playing when the app is in the background
WAKE_LOCK Prevent the device from sleeping during audio playback
Media Library (iOS) Access your on-device music library on iOS

Data Storage

All data used by Nen — including your preferences, favorites list, and recently played history — is stored locally on your device only. No data is ever transmitted off your device. Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

Third-Party Services

Nen does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The audio engine is powered by SoLoud (via flutter_soloud), which runs entirely on-device.

Children's Privacy

Nen does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
